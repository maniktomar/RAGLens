RAGLens AI Governance Standard

AI assistants must provide source citations when they answer policy, legal, security, finance, or customer-impacting questions. If the retrieved context does not contain enough evidence, the assistant must say it does not know instead of guessing.

High-risk AI workflows require human review before actions are taken. Examples include account termination, refunds above 5000 USD, contract changes, security exceptions, and regulatory filings.

Model usage is reviewed monthly. The review includes prompt logs, retrieval quality, hallucination reports, latency, estimated cost, and user feedback. Teams must document model changes before deploying them to production.
